Our History

American Shipping Line is an international licensed and registered freight forwarder. We are a full service transport company specializing in cargo shipping worldwide. Our core values for delivery are safety, punctuality, and efficiency.

These principles governs our performance towards your shipment. With many years of experience in all aspects of the import and export business worldwide, we are always ready to serve you to the best of our knowledge and ability.

How It Works

Through our networks and agents' efforts, we offer unique services for the following items:

1

Co-ordinating air or ocean cargo booking and tracking

2

Providing local pick-up, delivery and interstate trucking

3

Consolidating both air and ocean to and from anywhere in all 7 continents

4

Preparing necessary documentation such as bank letter of credit

5

Checking with overseas for cargo delivery to CNEE

6

Assisting customers with their proper paperwork and going through the legal processes to meet the rules and regulations of the custom

7

Supplying insurance policies and claims when necessary
